Future Projections with SSPs:

We ran the model under various Shared Socioeconomic Pathways (SSPs) such as SSP126, SSP245, SSP370, and SSP585, for two future time periods (2021-2040 and 2041-2060). These SSPs represent different greenhouse gas concentration trajectories and socioeconomic assumptions:
SSP126: Low emissions scenario, assuming a strong global shift toward sustainability.
SSP245: Moderate emissions scenario, assuming intermediate levels of mitigation and adaptation.
SSP370: Higher emissions scenario, with more regionalized approaches and fewer mitigation efforts.
SSP585: Very high emissions scenario, representing minimal mitigation efforts.
